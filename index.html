An accomplished engineer and architect who is passionate about the development of Rwanda. He was born and grew up as a refugee in Tanzania and moved back to Rwanda to complete his studies. Guiding Principles

We believe in people

We respect the dignity and worth of all people.

We keep it local

We believe that water, sanitation, and hygiene problems are most effectively solved using local resources.

We keep good company

We search out trusted partners who share our vision and work together to build long-term relationships based on trust.

We keep our promises

We believe we owe it to the communities we serve, our volunteers, staff and donors to act with integrity and manage our resources effectively and efficiently.

Our Mission

Aswanet works with people and partners to develop innovative and long-lasting solutions to the water, sanitation, and hygiene problems in the developing world. We strive to continually improve current technologies, to exepriment with promising new technologies, and to leverage resources to multiply our impact.

Frequently asked questions

Where are you based?

Our team is headquartered in Kigali, Rwanda, but we have satellite offices in Kampala, Uganda and New York City, USA.

How much will it cost?

Every solution is different, and our team will provide an appropriate estimate based on our experience and your needs.

How are you different from the others?

The Aswanet team approaches each project from a "beginning to end" mindset and determines which solution is best. The team are not technology providers; rather they put different technologies together to provide the optimal solution for your needs. The team combines international best practices with intimate local knowledge and experience.

I already have a utility connection but it's unreliable. Can you help?

Yes, our team has many solutions that will help with unreliable utility connections. Please contact us to schedule a site visit.

I'm planning a project or investment
in Africa and need help getting enough water. Can you help me?

Absolutely - please contact us below.
